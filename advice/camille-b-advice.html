Hi, my name is Camille and I have some advice for you!

Take things at your own pace!

We're all starting from a different place. I've found that just accepting that some things might take longer for me to grasp, while others will be easy, has been really helpful.

Some days you can breeze through several lessons at once. Other days it might take you several days to complete one lesson. Keep Going!

Find the fun!

You are so much more likely to stick to something if you find it fun. So far, I've 'found the fun' by adding custom, silly elements to my assignments.

Don't believe me? Think of the things you've tried in the past that were FUN and ended up being a huge part of your life. Maybe it was that team sport? Or the art class? Or spending time with your grandparents!

Keep looking on the fun and the lessons will be completed in no time!

Reward Objectives: big and small!

Logged into your account every day for a week? Get some ice-cream!

Finished an entire lesson? Go for a walk in a beautiful park!

Think of rewards you would love and use them to push yourself forward!
